I am an associate professor of economics at the Center for Industrial Economics (CERNA) at Mines Paris – PSL. I am also a Research Associate at ZEW Mannheim, a Research Member at the Paris Center for Law and Economics (CRED), Affiliate Researcher at WU Vienna, and a Research Affiliate at the CEPR.

My papers have been published in journals such as American Economic Journal: Microeconomics, Journal of Public Economics, Journal of Political Economy Microeconomics, and European Economic Review. I advise institutions like the European Commission and the German Federal Ministry of Economic Affairs, and serve as a court expert in cartel cases.
